Keep the whole car happy — without handing kids a screen to stare at. MileQuest turns your phone into a game master for car journeys, flights and train trips: it runs the games, keeps score, and gets everyone looking out the window, talking, and laughing together.

SCREEN-LIGHT BY DESIGN
The phone gives prompts and tracks points — the fun happens in the car, not on the display.

PLAY ANYWHERE — FULLY OFFLINE
No internet needed once installed. Perfect for airplane mode, remote motorways, and long train rides.

MADE FOR MIXED AGES
Add each traveler with their age and every game adapts. A 5-year-old gets prompts they can guess, teens get a real challenge — and everyone plays the same round together.

DRIVER-SAFE
The driver is automatically left out of any game that needs eyes on the screen, and included in games that don't.

• Trip Leaderboard — points, ranks, and bragging rights
